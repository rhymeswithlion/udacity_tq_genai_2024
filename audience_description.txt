The potential audience for this material includes business executives, managers, and professionals seeking an accessible yet comprehensive understanding of artificial intelligence (AI) and its impact on various industries. It is designed for individuals who may not have a technical background but need to grasp the fundamental concepts, applications, and ethical considerations of AI to make informed decisions. Use cases include corporate training sessions, executive briefings, and educational seminars where the goal is to demystify AI, explore its practical implications, and discuss strategies for integrating AI technologies into business operations effectively and responsibly.
